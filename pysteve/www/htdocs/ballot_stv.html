This is a ballot with N candidates and N seats available. The red line denotes the cutaway, should all your choices be voted in. All the nominees are placed in random order on the candidate list. If the candidate has prepared a statement, you can view it by clicking on the statement link to the right of the candidate's name.

How to vote:
Drag a candidate from the candidate list to the ballot box to place them in the vote. ORDER MATTERS: Put your candidates in the order of your preference, with the first preference at the top, the second below that and so on. You can rearrange your votes as you see fit, by dragging candidates up/down on the ballot box list. You may place as many candidates in the ballot box as you see fit. If you want to remove a single candidate from your ballot box, simply drag the candidate back to the list of remaining candidates to the left.
