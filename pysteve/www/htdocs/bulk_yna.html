Here you can perform a quick vote for each of the yes/no issues in this election. Click on yes, no or abstain to cast your vote. Your vote is cast and registered once you click the respective button, and can be changed at any time, as long as the election is still open. When you vote, an icon will appear next to the vote options to indicate the vote that was recorded by the system.

When you click on a button, your vote is registered immediately. There is no 'done' button here.
Also note that this page only lists Yes/No/Abstain issues. There may be other issues in need of your vote on the main election page.
